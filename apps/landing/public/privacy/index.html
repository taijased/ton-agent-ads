Privacy Policy

This policy explains what product and account data AdAgent may process when you use the landing site, Telegram bot, and Telegram Mini App to run campaign workflows.

Last updated: March 26, 2026 Focused on Telegram and Mini App workflows
Telegram Auth

Mini App access may rely on Telegram session data and validation of Telegram init data.

Campaign Operations

We process campaign inputs, workspace activity, and related records to operate the service you use.

Wallet Safety

Private keys and secret recovery phrases are not stored by AdAgent.

1. Information we may process

Depending on the product surface you use, AdAgent may process account identifiers, Telegram profile information, campaign details, channel references, negotiation records, approval actions, payment-related metadata, publication status, analytics, and technical usage data needed to operate the service.

2. Telegram Mini App authentication

When you open AdAgent as a Telegram Mini App, the product may receive Telegram-provided session data such as signed `initData` payloads and user profile fields supplied by Telegram. AdAgent may validate that payload in order to authenticate the session and issue an internal product session token.

This processing is used to confirm access, personalize the experience, and protect the integrity of account sessions.

3. Campaign and workspace data

If you create or manage campaigns through AdAgent, we may process the information you submit or generate in the workflow, including campaign goals, budgets, descriptions, shortlisted channels, conversation context, deal statuses, proofs, and analytics.

We use this information to run product features, keep campaign state consistent across the bot and Mini App, and provide reporting and operational visibility.

4. Wallet connections and payment metadata

If you connect a wallet, AdAgent may process wallet connection metadata, transaction-related references, and payment status data needed to support wallet-connected product flows.

AdAgent does not store wallet private keys, seed phrases, or equivalent wallet secrets. Wallet authorization and signing are handled through compatible third-party wallet tools.

5. How we use information

  • To authenticate users and secure sessions.
  • To provide campaign creation and campaign management flows.
  • To support negotiation, payment preparation, verification, and analytics features.
  • To improve product reliability, safety, and debugging.
  • To investigate abuse, misuse, or operational incidents when necessary.

6. Sharing and third-party boundaries

AdAgent may rely on third-party platforms and infrastructure that are necessary to operate the product, including Telegram and compatible wallet providers. Information visible inside those external services is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

7. Retention and security

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, maintain product integrity, resolve disputes, investigate abuse, and meet legal or operational obligations.

AdAgent uses reasonable technical and operational safeguards, but no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed as perfectly secure.

8. Your choices and updates

You may stop using the service at any time. Because some product access depends on Telegram session context, closing the Mini App or disconnecting wallet access may affect available features.

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Continued use of AdAgent after an update means you accept the current published version of this policy.
